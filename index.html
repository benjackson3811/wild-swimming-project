What is Wild Swimming? Wild Swimming is open water swimming, outside in the embrace of nature. You don't have to be in a swimming pool to Wild Swimming, you just need to desire to go swimming where you desire. Anyone of any age can go wild swimming. You just need to be safe!

Benefits

  • Open Spaces
  • Expanding Your Social Circle
  • Being at one with Nature
  • Physically and Mentally Challenging
  • Increase Energy
  • Weight loss
  • Minimise Pain
  • Immune System Boost
  • Personal Achievement
  • Its loads of Fun
